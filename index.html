What is Digital Art?

Digital Art is an evolution of traditonal art, made on an electronic device. It allows you to ssave your work as an image file on your coputer. So what is this the difference between digital art and traditonal, and what makes it special? First off, art is not limited by just 2 types, it could be anything that you created. Digital art is just pieces of artwork made using softwares, computers, tablets, or other pieces of technology. Some examples would be: animations, photographs, or illustrations.

What is the difference between digital art and traditonal art?

Traditional Art is what you would call artwork made from actual drawing supplies, like paper and pencil. There are many different medias, which gives artists a lot of options to choose from, and everyone has preferences.
